About Our Student Marks Portal

Welcome to our Student Marks Portal, a comprehensive platform designed to simplify the process of managing student marks and generating custom results. Our portal provides a user-friendly interface to upload CSV files containing student responses and answer keys, allowing you to generate accurate and personalized results effortlessly.

Key Features:

  • Easy CSV Upload: Simply upload CSV files with student responses and answer keys.
  • Custom Result Generation: Generate personalized results based on uploaded data.
  • Student Question Analysis: Gain insights into individual student performance and question-wise analysis.
